What is the difference between Bootstrap 4 and Bootstrap 5

Bootstrap5

  • It has 6 default responsive tiers i.e. xs, sm, md, lg, xl, and xxl.
  • Bootstrap 5 has expanded color support.
  • Bootstrap 5 does not support Internet Explorer 10 and 11.
  • In Bootstrap 5 you will find its own SVG icons.
  • The columns can not be placed relatively.

Bootstrap4

  • It has 5 default responsive tiers i.e. xs, sm, md, lg, and xl.
  • Bootstrap 4 has limited color support.
  • Bootstrap 4 does support Internet Explorer 10 and 11 both.
  • Bootstrap 4 does not have its own SVG icons.
  • In Bootstrap 4, the columns can be placed and positioned relatively.
