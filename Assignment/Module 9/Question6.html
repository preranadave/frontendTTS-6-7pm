What is Header and Content Components in React Js?

Header components are used to display the website's brand and navigation. They include website's logo, and a menu of links. Header components can also include other search bar, social media icons, or a call to action button. Content components are responsible to dislay the main body of the page. It has text, images,map etc. Content components used different types of pages, such as blog, product pages, or contact us, about us pages.
